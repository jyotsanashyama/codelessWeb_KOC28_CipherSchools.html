About Us

RAINBOW PALESTRA HEALTH CLUB was established in the year 1990 August 5th.We started our journey with some barbell, weight plates, dumbbells, and only one bench. Time to time improvement and development are our achievements. In the field of bodybuilding, our athletes own many medals in state bodybuilding championship along with Mr. North Bihar 2007, Junior Mr. Bihar 2003 and many times Mr Darbhanga district title. We are famous for special women's fitness, weight gain and weight loss within target period.
